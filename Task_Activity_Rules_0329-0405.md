## 6. Legal Terms

### Activity A — Buy 3 Drinks, Get 50% Off

1. This promotion ("Promotion") runs from March 29, 2025 to April 5, 2025 (Eastern Time). Purchase three (3) handcrafted drinks during the Promotion period to receive one (1) 50% Off Coupon ("Coupon").

2. Only purchases of handcrafted beverages ("Drinks") at participating LUCKIN COFFEE™ locations count toward task completion. Toppings, customizations, food items, and merchandise do not qualify.

3. A qualifying purchase is counted toward task progress only after the order has been picked up and confirmed (i.e., upon successful scan at pickup). Orders that are not picked up or are canceled do not count.

4. Each customer may complete this task and receive the Coupon once only.

5. Upon task completion, the Coupon will be automatically issued to the customer's account. The Coupon is valid for seven (7) days from the date of issuance and expires thereafter.

6. The Coupon applies a 50% discount to one (1) handcrafted drink per order and cannot be combined with any other promotions, discounts, or coupons.

7. The Coupon may be automatically applied if there are qualifying items in the cart; you can manually remove it at checkout.

8. The Coupon is non-refundable, non-transferable, and has no cash value.

9. Luckin Coffee reserves the right to disqualify any customer who engages in fraud, abuse, or misuse of these terms.

10. By participating in this Promotion, you agree to be bound by LUCKIN COFFEE™'s Terms of Use, available at www.luckincoffee.us/terms-of-use.
